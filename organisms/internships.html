About the Internship Programme

The internship programme at Hilleman Laboratories offers a unique opportunity to develop personal and professional skills in a global company to kick-start your career in the biopharmaceutical industry.

As a part of the internship, students would gain practical skills through hands-on training opportunities in the labs and guidance from industry experts to kick start your career development.

Upon successful completion of the programme, there may be opportunities for you to continue full-time employment with Hilleman. To qualify for the internship, you must be enrolled in full-time studies in a tertiary institution and be able to commit to at least a three-month internship duration.

Interested students may send in a detailed CV to hr@hilleman-labs.org

Hilleman’s internship programme is aimed at helping students bridge their academic knowledge and skills with real-world applications in the industry.

We really enjoy having these young talents with us and I hope their time here inspires them to pursue a career in vaccine and biologics development.
